Attachment

Purpose  

         To add and delete episode's additional material information.

           Steps to proceed

1.      Click on the Program Management > Manage Episode Details > Episode Details > Attachment menu item. 

2.      To add new Attachment, right click at the Attachment section and select Edit, right click at the Attachment section again and select Add, enter the values in modifiable fields then click the save button.   

3.      To modify Attachment, select the media from the browse list, right click at the Attachment section and select Edit, retype over any modifiable fields and click the save button . 

4.      To delete a Attachment, select the media from the browse list, right click at the Attachment section and select Edit, right click again at the Attachment section and select delete. 

        Note: Deletions are not possible when the records in question are already used in relation to any other entities in the application.
