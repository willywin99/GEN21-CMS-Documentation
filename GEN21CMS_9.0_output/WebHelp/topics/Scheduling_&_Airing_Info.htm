Scheduling & Airing Info

Used To view the all important Scheduling information to the QC department so that they could plan, perform and deliver the mandatory pre-airing output from QC in the most effective and timely manner.  Here the QC department is able to view (only) all scheduling-related information, in the form of both past and future airing commitment(s) of the materials. 

 

 Step Scheduling & Airing Info

1.      Login to Gen21CMS.

2.      Open the technical (Quality Control-> Technical).

3.   On Media Usage History sub module, click button Scheduling & Airing Info.
