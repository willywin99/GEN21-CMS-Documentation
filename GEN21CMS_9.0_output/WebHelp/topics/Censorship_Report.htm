Censorship Report

Used to review and print the output of the Censorship information recording performed in earlier section.

Step Censorship Report

1.      Login to Gen21CMS.

2.      Open the technical (Quality Control-> Technical).

3.      On Transcription Details sub module, click button Censorship Report.
