Used to input and maintain necessary Censorship information of a media (Status is pending):

1.      The administration detail of the censorship (STLS, BKRV, dates etc)

2.    The detailed physical location of the cuts

3.    The possible cost associated with the censorship.

 

 Step Censorship Detail

1.  Login to Gen21CMS.

2.   Open the technical menu (Quality Control -> Technical).

3.   On Transcription Details sub module, click Censorship Details.
