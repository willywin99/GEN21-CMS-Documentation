Purpose

This is a common, general purpose and user-friendly maintenance facility for users who have less access rights to information (e.g. Scheduling, Traffic or Media Library department staff) to go directly to the detailed episode level screen that has the most frequently required information for them to view and make limited changes on.

Episode details have following sub modules:

  • Dubbing 
  • Subtitling 
  • Synopsis 
  • Stars Info  
  • Awards 
  • Attribute 
  • Censorship 
  • Tapes Info  
  • Runs/Plays 
  • Pre/Req 
  • Episode Cost
  • Attachment 
  • Target Audience  
  • Song Info  

Please refer to Program Purchase Contract .
