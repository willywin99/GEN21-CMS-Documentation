The second area of interest (transcription/content) is fully facilitated here (and in the next section, the Censorship). This is where transcription users can perform the following on a media. Input and maintain the necessary segmentation information ie. the physical location information of the media Optionally input any recommendations with regards to the defined segments. Produces the desired output of the exercise ie. the 'Program Cue Sheet'.

Step Proceed

 1.     Click on the Segmentation & Recommendation menu item in the left vertical subwindow menu. The information is displayed in 3 subwindows (observe the subwindow titlenames) and in 2 tabs at the bottom of the page;

-   The 'Program Cue Sheet' tab at the bottom will be invisible if Technical QC Form   dataentry has not been performed. This is due to the fact that some information in the Program Cue Sheet are coming from the Technical QC Form.

- Please note that there may be multiple lines appearing on all 3 subwindows depending on defined content ie. multiple episodes, segmentations and recommendations. Position your cursor on each listed line (episode, segmentation) to view their individual recommendation(s).

2.         Proceed with dataentry by first selecting the episode to be worked on, then rightclick at either the 'Media Segmentation' or the 'Transcription Recommendation' subwindow then select 'Edit' to enter edit mode. Perform another rightclick but exactly at the desired subwindow, then select 'Add'. A single blank line in that subwindow should appear, ready to accept inputs.

3.        Enter all details on individual line(s). When a line is done, rightclick and select 'Add' at the menu to add more lines, then click on the 'Save'   button when all lines have been entered. Alternately, one may perform a 'Save' everytime a line is completed. Pls note that validation is triggered by the Save action.

4.         Preview of the Program Cue Sheet output is always available by clicking on the 'Program Cue Sheet' tab. There one review the inputs and finally print the sheet when read

5.         Always end activities with a save button click
