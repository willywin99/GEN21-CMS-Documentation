Metadata is "data that provides information about other data". In other words, it is "data about data". Many distinct type of metadata exist, including descriptive metadata, structural metadata, administrative metadata, reference metadata and statistical metadata.

Steps Metadata

  1. Login to Gen21CMS.
  2. Open Episode Details (Program Management > Manage Episode > Episode Details).
  3. Field the Search Parameter / Find the PO.
  4. Click Search.
  5. From the Left Section click Metadata.
