Used to register the names of all people who are performing transcription/QC activities, regardless whether they are accessing the system or not (ie. these are NOT login ids). This is necessary since many of these activities are done outside the system and recording of information are centralized under 1 or more appointed QC administrators.  Also department names of all users need to be associated with the people, to allow proper groupings by the system (ie. users from technical/QC or transcription/content-related groups).

    Open the Transcription/QC Uses

  1. Login to Gen21CMS.
  2. Open the Application Configuration (Quality Control -> Parameter -> Transcription/QC Users).

Steps Adding New Transcription/QC Users

1. Open Transcription/QC Users

2. Create new Transcription/QC Users,

      a. Right click on mouse button on the header field and choose

          “add row” to create new Transcription/QC Users

      b. Define the Code, Name and Dept.

      c. Right click on mouse button and choose “save”.

Steps Editing Transcription /QC Users

1. Open Transcription/QC Users

2. Edit specific Transcription/QC Users,

      a. Right click on mouse button and choose “edit mode”.

      b. Edit the Name and Dept.

      c. Right click on mouse button and choose “save”.

Steps Deleting Transcription/QC Users

1. Open Transcription/QC Users

2. Delete Transcription/QC Users,

      a. Right click on mouse button and choose “edit mode”.

      b. Right click on mouse button and choose “delete”.
