Area / Department

 

Purpose               

Creation and maintenance of Department/Cost Centre definition.

 

Steps to proceed

1.      Click on the Program Management > Manage Episode Details > Parametes > Area / Department menu item. 

2.      To add new Area / Department, right click at the header and select Edit, right click at the header again and select Add, enter the values in modifiable fields then click the save button. 

3.      To modify Area / Department, select the media from the browse list, right click at the header of Form tab and select Edit, retype over any modifiable fields and click the save button .  

To delete Area / Department, select the media from the browse list, right click at the header and select Edit, right click again at the header and select delete. 

Note: Deletions are not possible when the records in question are already used in relation to any other entities in the application.
